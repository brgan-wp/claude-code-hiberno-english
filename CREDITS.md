# Credits

## Spinner verbs

The Irish spinner verbs are **not** part of this repository. The installer pulls
them at install time from:

- **[tabman83/claude-code-spinner-verbs-irish](https://github.com/tabman83/claude-code-spinner-verbs-irish)** — © tabman83, MIT License.

His pack is the source of truth for the loading-spinner words. We fetch his
`spinner-verbs.json` as data and merge it into your `~/.claude/settings.json`
ourselves; we never redistribute or vendor a copy of his list. If you want only
the spinner and not the language, install his pack directly from the link above.

The Hiberno-English output style and glossary in this repository are original to
